Fair and affordable pricing

Photo credit: Benoît Meunier on Flickr

Pay only for what you use; scale as you grow!

We don't believe in confusing plans or feature locking. These strategies bait you with a low monthly price only to place you in a much higher tier once you start growing. Our goal is to be transparent in our pricing and affordable for ministries of any size. To this end, you are billed only for the features you use.

Our pricing continues to be under development as sections of the platform move through the beta process. We welcome your continued feedback!

Bandwidth

Bandwidth is the data that gets passed to a viewer when they watch a video or listen to an audio file. Every time someone presses play, data is passed from our servers to their browsers, which counts as bandwidth.

10GB of bandwidth is roughly the equivelant of ~10,000 minutes of audio or ~1,500 minutes of video. You are billed based on the total amout of data used each month. Have questions?
