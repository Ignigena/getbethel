Equipping the digital Church

Cutting edge and automated open source technology built for the Church.

An open source platform for the Church.

Bethel makes managing your church's digital needs faster and easier. Designed with features for churches of all sizes: whether you're a new church plant or a large multi-campus church. No contracts or expensive licensing fees. We believe the Church should have access to the greatest technology available, so we're building a cutting edge ministry platform to do just that.

Everything's Integrated

One platform integrating the digital needs for all of your campuses. Manage multiple campuses all in one place and save valuable time.

Designed for Teams

Everyone on your team can access the platform with tailored permissions for staff members or volunteers based on the campus they serve.

Power in the Cloud

No software to install, updates happen automatically. Designed with an API-first approach to integrate with everything.
