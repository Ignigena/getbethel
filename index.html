An open source platform for the Church.

Bethel makes managing your church's digital needs faster and easier.
It's designed with features for churches of all sizes whether you're a new church plant or a large multi-campus church.

Everything's integrated

One platform integrating the digital needs for all of your campuses. Manage everything all in one place.

Designed for teams

Everyone in your team can access the platform with tailored permissions for each staff member or volunteer.

Power in the cloud

No software to install, updates happen automatically. Designed with an API-first approach to integrate with everything.
